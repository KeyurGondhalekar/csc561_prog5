Prog 5 final submission


you can use "a" and "d" to move left/right
Using "Space" will fire missiles from your ship.

The enemy ships come down in pairs of 2 and fire missiles downwards.
If an enemy or their missile hits your ship, it's Game Over!

If you destroy all the enemy ships, you win!

The Make it your own is started by pressing the "!" key

That'll make the visual style of the game look like old vector games!
